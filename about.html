About me




Hello again! My name is Hemant Gupta. I live in Bangalore and study Interaction Design, master programme, at National Institute of Design (India).

As a defence kid, I have travelled across India, and I love exploring new places, meeting new people and listening to instrumental music. Now, I often find myself fiddling with my new pen tablet so that I can learn doing illustrations digitally. I also have this knack of learning new things quickly and most of the web related work I have learnt in recent past.

Prior to coming to NID, I was working in Gurgaon as Software Engineer for 04 years in the network managment system (NMS) domain and have worked with clients like Ciena, Alcatel-Lucent, ABB and Aricent Group.

Beyond work, I enjoy trekking with friends, playing table tennis and badminton the most, and have made really good friends while playing sports.

If you need to get in touch or learn more about what I do, just drop in an email.
